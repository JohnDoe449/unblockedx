Welcome to FCPS Maryland

Frederick County Public Schools (FCPS) is a dynamic and student‑centered school system dedicated to providing high‑quality education for every learner. Our schools are built on a foundation of academic excellence, innovation, and strong community support.

Why FCPS Is a Valuable Place to Learn

FCPS offers a learning environment where students are encouraged to think critically, explore new ideas, and develop the skills they need for future success. With a focus on both academic achievement and personal growth, our schools help students build confidence, creativity, and resilience through meaningful opportunities.

Strong Academics

Students benefit from a rigorous curriculum designed to challenge them at every level. FCPS provides high‑quality instruction in core subjects, along with opportunities for advanced coursework, STEM programs, arts education, and hands‑on learning experiences.

Innovative Learning

Classrooms across the county are equipped with modern technology that supports collaboration and exploration. Students engage in project‑based learning, use digital tools to solve real‑world problems, and participate in programs that prepare them for college and careers.

Supporting the Whole Child

FCPS is committed to creating safe, inclusive, and supportive environments. Students have access to counseling, wellness programs, and social‑emotional learning resources that help them grow not only academically but personally.

Pathways to the Future

Whether students plan to attend college, enter the workforce, or pursue technical training, FCPS provides pathways that help them reach their goals. Career and technology programs, internships, and partnerships with local organizations give students valuable real‑world experience.
